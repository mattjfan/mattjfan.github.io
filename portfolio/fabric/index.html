Fabric

Web Development | Solo | 2017

Fabric is a simple ASCII art editor I built during Winter Break 2017.

The project was an easy way to play more with HTML and CSS, especially more esoteric attributes, like contenteditable. Also gave me a chance to play with Jquery, and get better acclimated to working with Javascript.
