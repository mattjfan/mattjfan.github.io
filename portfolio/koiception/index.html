Koiception

Digital Art | Solo | 2017

Koiception is a large format digital art piece that I created during winter break, 2017.

The piece was created in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, with the vectors being created in Illustrator, and shading and noise being added later in photoshop. To allow for a high working resolution and reduce lag, each of the smaller 'ponds' were actually processed in photoshop individually, and then composited into the larger image.
