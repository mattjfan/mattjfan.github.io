Kodactive

UI & UX Design | Team | 2017

I joined the ed-tech startup Kodactive as the lead Product Designer, designing user experience and creating concept sketches for the user interface.

Kodactive was an ed-tech startup creating STEM toys to teach kids programming. Our first product was an RC car that could be programmed with a drag-and-drop coding interface, which transitioned into a more formal programming language as kids progressed through the curriculum.

During my time on the team, we were finalists at UMD's Do Good Challenge, Winners at the CS department's FishBowl pitch competition, and accepted into the Dingman Center Hatch Accelerator.

Kodactive is currently inactive.
