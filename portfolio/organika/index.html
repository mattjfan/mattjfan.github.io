Organika

Digital Art & Illustration | Solo | 2016

Organika and Organika 2 were two digital art series I made for NeonMob, a digital art collection platform.

Combined, the series had over 30,000 copies of digital art claimed by collectors through the site.

The series were produced using traditional media with pen and ink, and markers. For Organika 2, I introduced digital post-processing in photoshop for the backgrounds, and to touch up problem areas.
