Bespoke Investment Group Internship

Software Development & Design | Team | 2018

I worked at Bespoke Investment Group Summer 2018, as a front-end intern on a two-person development team. I redesigned a new conversions oriented landing page, designed and built a web application from scratch to provide a better, interactive user experience with data from our legacy WordPress site, created algorithms to automate traditionally human tasks such as trend classification and dynamic summary generation, and designed assets for one of our joint ventures, Premo Social.
