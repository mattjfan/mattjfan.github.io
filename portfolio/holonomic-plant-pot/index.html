Holonomic Plant Pot

Hardware & Electronics | Solo | 2018

I designed and built a light-following, holonomic plant pot system using AVR microcontrollers and other low-cost and 3d printed parts. The project is still in development, but you can check out the source and parts files here, and see a video I made talking about and demoing the system for my ENME351 class here.
